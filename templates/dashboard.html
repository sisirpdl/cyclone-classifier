"Hurricanes are tropical storms that form over the North Atlantic Ocean and Northeast Pacific. Cyclones are formed over the South Pacific and Indian Ocean. Typhoons are formed over the Northwest Pacific Ocean." ~ BBC News
The cyclones/hurricanes/typhoons are irregular wind movements involving closed air circulation around a low pressure centre. This closed air circulation is caused by atmospheric disturbances over and above the earth’s surface, coupled with the earth’s rotation which imparts to these disturbances a whirling motion.

You can upload an image of cyclone/hurricane/typhoon and predict its speed and ultimately classify it as less/more destructive. The detailed are highlighted more after an image file submission.
